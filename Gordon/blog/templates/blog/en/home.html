A Bit About Me

Gordon Zinoviy Leontievich — mining engineer, mine-builder.

I was born on 18 January 1936, in Stalino (Donetsk), Ukraine. For about 40 years I worked in the coal industry. I started my career as a shift engineer in a mining site, and ended it as Deputy General Director for production of the All-Union Association "Spetsshakhtoburenie". I was not a member of the party, and not because I didn't want to be. Among my various awards, the most dear to me are the "Miner's Glory" 1st, 2nd, and 3rd degree. I have the fondest memories of the people I lived and worked with. Married. Three daughters and three grandchildren.
